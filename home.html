Welcome to the Lisle Woman's Club Website



We are a member of GFWC that has a long history of philanthropy, social and community leadership. The Lisle Woman's Club founded the Lisle Library in 1964 and we continue to support our community food pantry, women's services, veterans assistance and local scholarships.
