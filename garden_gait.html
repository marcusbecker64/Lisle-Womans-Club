Garden Gait is our premier fundraising activity, showcasing delightful homeowner gardens in Lisle. Starting at Lisle Depot station, have refreshments and visit our craft fair vendors. This event supports our scholarship program. We are especially proud to feature The Growing Place this year where you can purchase annuals and perennials for your own garden!
