Lisle Women's Club Goals and Accomplishments

 Scholarships - Available to residents of Lisle high school students and continuing education for women returning to college.
 Lisle Library - Contributing to and supporting Friends of the Library.
 Local Food Pantry - Providing holiday gifts, baskets of food and gift certificates to families in need.
 International Affairs - Supporting efforts to improve the lives of women and children around the globe.
 Conservation

Lisle Woman’s Club volunteers over 12,000 hours throughout the community annually for such organizations as:


• Women’s Choice Services

Hosting a fun baby shower in support of women and their babies
• Save a Child

Knit red hats for low weight babies
• Heifer Organization

Raise money for Chickens
Pigs, Sheep, Water Buffalo and Llama
• Christmas Presents

Donated Presents and food items for local families at Christmastime
• Operation Stand Down

Sock/scarf collection during the month of October that benefits Veterans
In one year 500 books were donated to the Food Pantry to Feed a Child’s Imagination

Over 45 years of giving
